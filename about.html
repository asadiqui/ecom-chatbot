Our Story

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Zehrat Bladi has been bringing joy and beauty to our community through carefully crafted floral arrangements. What started as a passion for flowers has grown into a trusted local business that serves weddings, celebrations, and everyday moments of beauty.

Our name "Zehrat Bladi" means "Flower of My Country" in Arabic, reflecting our commitment to celebrating both local and international floral traditions. We believe that flowers have the power to express emotions, create memories, and transform spaces.

Our Commitment

Whether you're celebrating a milestone, expressing sympathy, or simply brightening someone's day, we're here to help you find the perfect floral expression.
